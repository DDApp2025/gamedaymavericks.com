About Gameday Mavericks

A Las Vegas card shop built by collectors, for collectors. Sports cards, Pokemon, and TCG under one roof.

Our Story

For the love of the hobby

Gameday Mavericks started with a simple idea: give Las Vegas a real home base for collectors. A place where you can browse loaded cases, rip packs with friends, and get an honest deal whether you are buying your first box or selling a collection you have built for years.

We carry sealed product, graded slabs, and singles across every major sport, Pokemon, and the top trading card games. New inventory hits the shelves every week, and our live breaks bring the whole crew together.

Stop in, talk cards, and let us help you find your next pull.
